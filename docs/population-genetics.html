Landscape Genetic Data Analysis

4 Population Genetics

The lecture content for Day 2 will focus on developing a basis in Population Genetic theory. It is the intentions of this content to provide as much as is necessary for understanding how genetic data are used in the application of landscape genetic studies.

The field of population genetics is broad and extends well beyond the issues that landscape genetics is interested in examining. That said, there is much more within the established field of population genetics that can be applied to the much younger suite of landscape genetic studies.

For more information on general population genetic background and analyses there are a host of great texts available focusing on the theoretical backgrounds. For a hands-on experience, I recommend my text, Applied Population Genetics (another Creative Commons text like this one and a shameless plug!).
